Commands

Command Description
/tpa <player> Send a teleport request to another player.
/tpahere <player> Request that a player teleport to you.
/tpaccept [player] Accept a teleport request.
/tpdeny [player] Deny a teleport request.
/tpacancel Cancel your outgoing teleport request.
/order Place buy or sell orders for items.
/sell Sell an item to the server or market.
/ah Open the Auction House interface.
/duel <player> Challenge another player to a duel.
/settings Opens Settings.
/spawn Teleport to the spawn location.

Economy & Currency

The Donut SMP economy is player-driven and competitive. Money is used to buy, sell, and trade items, while Shards can be earned through kills, AFK rewards, or events.

  • Sell items with /sell or via the /ah Auction House.
  • Use /order to place buy/sell orders for specific items.
  • Some items require Shards instead of cash.

About Donut SMP

Donut SMP is a competitive survival Minecraft server featuring an economy, teleport requests, PvP, and duels. Players can build, trade, and battle for dominance. Always check the official donutsmp.net for the latest updates and rule changes.
